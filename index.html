Welcome to the Stayin Alive Game

Collect all diamonds on the game grid. Every diamond will give you one extra second. Once you have collected diamonds, a KEY will appear on the map. Grab the KEY. Once you grab the KEY, an EXIT will appear. Head out to the EXIT. But hurry, you only have 20 seconds to get out or you will DIE! At Every Level increase, speed gets faster and less starting time is provided.
